ABOUT US

This prestigious temple of education “Renaissance Public School” was established in 2001, and is a pioneer in promoting all round development and to foster an all round growth in its wards.

The true essence of school lies , not in its infrastructure of four walls but its overall purpose of imparting quality education which includes the art of shaping individual’s personality through physical, emotional, intellectual and spiritual development.

The school devotes itself diligently to grooming of students in such a way that they excel in all walks of life, make a mark in every field of human activity and become proud citizens. Our school is well-equipped to ensure benchmarked learning, total focus on child and has a productive environment to ensure every child’s happiness.

VISION & MISSION

EDUCATE TO ELEVATE
“Nurturing every individual through high quality education and imbibing universal values”

Theme of the institution is dedicated to the values and believes of the Father of nation Mahatma Gandhi and their relevance to today’s world.

Through the School, we promise that our students get valuable insights into the teachings of Mahatma Gandhi, and we make an active effort to imbibe these values into our students.

We at RPS have signature six senses of values among our students - Simplicity, Honesty, Self-Determination, Perseverance, Self-Sufficiency and Self-Discipline.

OUR CAMPUSES

Renaissance is situated across 2 campuses one in Vaishali and one upcoming campus in Ghaziabad. Both campuses are easily accessible to the residents of Delhi and Ghaziabad and nearby areas. The Vaishali campus can also be accessed by metro and Ghaziabad campus can be accessed using various public transports.
